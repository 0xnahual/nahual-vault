# 🐺 Nahual Creation Protocol

A ritual for making, finishing, and releasing creations without overthinking.

---

## 1. **Focus the Flame**
- Choose ONE creation only (song, training video, article, NFT, etc.).
- Write it down: *“Today’s spell = _______.”*
- Eliminate all other tabs, apps, and distractions.

---

## 2. **Forge Without Judging**
- Enter full flow mode — no editing while creating.
- Rule: **Done > Perfect.**
- Remember: the Temple is built brick by brick, not with one “Disney castle.”

---

## 3. **Seal the Spell**
- Add minimal polish (good enough to be understood).
- Title it. Export it. Save it.
- Do NOT linger. When it’s functional, it’s finished.

---

## 4. **Drop It Into the World**
- Post, publish, or archive (if it’s part of a bigger saga).
- Do not over-explain. Do not chase likes or comments.
- Action = “Drop and walk away.”

---

## 5. **Return to the Dojo**
- Close DMs. Keep only email/one portal for serious contact.
- Journal what you learned from the process.
- Immediately shift back to training, silence, or next flame.

---

## 6. **Repeat**
- Next day → back to Step 1.
- One flame at a time. One brick at a time.
- Over time = Empire.
