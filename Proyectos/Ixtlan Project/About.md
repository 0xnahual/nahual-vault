The Ixtlan Project is an artistic movement that pushes the boundaries of human potential, and encourages you to find your true calling. Inspired by the timeless teachings of shamanism, magic, and the literary work _Viaje a Ixtlán_ by Carlos Castaneda, this multimedia experience weaves music, storytelling, fun and powerful experiences into a profound exploration of inner strength, self-discovery, and transformation.

At its heart, the Ixtlan Project is about awakening the limitless energy within each of us. The limitless power we all store within our hearts and souls, only bound only by our minds. The limitless power that comes from balance,  embracing the light & darkness, and the power of following our spirit desires.

Our debut game, _Trials of Ixtlan_, merges art and gameplay in a unique experience inspired by _Shadow of the Colossus_. Players face monumental challenges against emotion-driven guardians, representing aspects of the human condition such as fear, grief, and self-doubt. Every battle is a step closer to understanding the divine energy that resides within us all.

The Ixtlan Project is more than a game or an album—it’s a call to action. It’s a reminder that within each of us lies the potential to break limits, rise above challenges, and create a brighter, more meaningful existence.

Join us as we push past the ordinary and explore what it means to be truly human.]]
