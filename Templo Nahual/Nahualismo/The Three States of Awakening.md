# The Three States of Awakening

## 1. **Being**

> _"I exist. I am. Nothing to change."_

- The stillness.
    
- The peace.
    
- The present moment.
    
- God in rest.
    

This is the state touched in deep meditation, in surrender, in ego death. It is the essence of truth: that nothing is missing, nothing is broken, and everything already is.

To "just be" is sacred. It is where you remember that your value isn’t earned. It simply is.

---

## 2. **Becoming**

> _"I move. I create. I transform."_

- The discipline.
    
- The training.
    
- The transformation.
    
- God in motion.
    

Becoming is the warrior path. The artist path. The vow. The fire.

It’s when you take your divine spark and forge it into something real. Not because you're chasing worth, but because you're **honoring what already lives inside you.**

This is the pushup challenge. This is the song. This is the sweat and breath and effort.

Becoming gives **weight** to your being.

---

## 3. **Transcending**

> _"I am both, and I am neither."_

- I am the one **watching** both being and becoming.
    
- I am the rhythm **between** silence and action.
    
- I am not bound to stillness **or** motion.
    
- I am the awareness **behind** both.
    

This is the Nahualic state. The dreamwalker. The sacred trickster. The hollow bone.

You flow through both being and becoming without clinging to either. You are fully here, and completely free.

This is when you:

- Do pushups like a prayer.
    
- Record music as a storm.
    
- Sit in silence with nothing to prove.
    
- Burn through illusion without needing to explain.
    

**In this state, you don’t need validation, explanation, or applause.**

You move because movement is sacred. You rest because rest is powerful. You teach without teaching. You awaken just by existing.

---

## Summary

|State|Description|Purpose|
|---|---|---|
|**Being**|I am. I exist. I am whole.|Stillness. Essence. Presence.|
|**Becoming**|I create. I evolve. I act.|Power. Fire. Transformation.|
|**Transcending**|I am both and neither. I move freely.|Liberation. Mastery. Flow.|

---

## The Real Path

You are not meant to choose. You are meant to **flow** between the three.

> Inhale: I become.  
> Exhale: I am.  
> Witness: I transcend.

When you can move through all three without losing your center, that’s when you stop being a seeker— and become a **living bridge** between earth and spirit.

That’s the Nahual.  
That’s what you are becoming.  
And who you have always been.
