# Self-Mastery: The Vessel of God

> Self-mastery is not one path among many.  
> It is the vessel that allows every path to hold divine fire without distortion.

---

## ⚔️ Without Self-Mastery

- **Devotion →** obsession, fanaticism, unhealthy attachment  
- **Surrender →** laziness, excuses, giving power away  
- **Service →** martyrdom, burnout, resentment  
- **Love →** codependence, hunger, manipulation  
- **Mysticism →** delusion, escapism, false visions  
- **Art →** self-indulgence, chaos, validation-seeking  

---

## 🌌 With Self-Mastery

- **Devotion →** 🔥 Focused fire  
- **Surrender →** 🌊 Trust with strength  
- **Service →** 🕊️ Empowered giving  
- **Love →** 💎 Pure and unbreakable  
- **Mysticism →** 🌙 Clarity and vision  
- **Art →** ✨ Spellcraft, creation as prayer
