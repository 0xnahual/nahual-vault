# 🌍 The Law of Rooted Movement

**Core Principle:**  
All power comes from the ground.  
You do not move yourself — you push or pull the world, and your body rises as a consequence.

---

## 1. Push Patterns

- **Push-Up** → Push the *floor* away with chest and arms.  
- **Step-Up / Stairs** → Push the *floor* away with glutes and legs.  
- **Punch (Jab / Cross)** → Drive the *foot* into the ground, let power spiral through hips/torso into fist.  

🔑 Cue: *Don’t think “lift yourself up” — think “press the earth away.”*

---

## 2. Pull Patterns

- **Pull-Up** → Pull the *bar* down to the ground, body rises as consequence.  
- **Row** → Pull the *weight / floor* toward you, not just “move elbows back.”  
- **Deadlift** → Push the *floor* away with legs as you drag the bar up.  

🔑 Cue: *Every pull begins by rooting, not by muscling.*

---

## 3. Rooting in Everyday Life

- Walking stairs = glutes pushing the earth.  
- Standing up from chair = quads/glutes driving floor away.  
- Carrying bags = lats pulling bar down, legs pushing ground.  

The body always follows the same law.

---

## 4. Why the Weighted Vest Unlocks This

- The vest magnifies gravity.  
- With extra load, inefficient movement collapses fast.  
- It forces awareness of how to **root → transfer → rise**.  
- Once you learn with the vest, unweighted movement feels effortless and powerful.

---

## 5. Martial Arts Application

- **Stance (Ma Bu / Gong Bu):** Root into the floor, relax upper body.  
- **Punches:** Ground → foot → hip → spine → fist.  
- **Kicks:** Ground → support leg drives floor away → strike leg releases.  

Every strike is a conversation with gravity.

---

## 🧙 Nahual Teaching

**Law:** Root → Transfer → Rise.  
- Root into the ground.  
- Transfer force through the body.  
- Rise or strike as a consequence.  

When you embody this, movement becomes flow, strength becomes effortless, and the body turns into a channel for energy.
