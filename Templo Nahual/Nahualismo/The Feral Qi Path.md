# ⚔️ The Feral Qi Path  
### Nahual Training System for Embodied Power, Perception, and Purpose

---

## 🕯️ Purpose

The **Feral Qi Path** is a structured daily training ritual for those who have accessed altered states of awareness—particularly primal, instinct-driven energy during plant medicine, breathwork, or spontaneous spiritual experience.

It is designed to:
- Stabilize the **beast state** (feral awareness, instinctual clarity)  
- Build and retain **Qi** (life force)  
- Transmute sexual and creative energy into grounded power  
- Strengthen the body as a vessel for **energetic presence**  
- Awaken the **Nahual archetype**: the one who walks between spirit and flesh, silence and sound, chaos and form

This is **not** breathwork for relaxation.  
This is **ritual technology** for warriors, creators, and sorcerers.

---

## 🧭 Daily Protocol Overview (30–45 minutes)

The practice includes:

1. Grounding  
2. Breath & Energy Activation  
3. Feral Movement Training  
4. Energy Lock-In  
5. Intent Programming

This is to be done **once per day**. Preferably in the **morning**, or before performance or ceremony.

---

## 🔻 1. Grounding (5 Minutes)

**Purpose**: To connect the energetic body to the Earth. To anchor before amplifying.

**Instructions**:
- Stand barefoot on natural surface (stone, dirt, grass, or raw wood)  
- Keep spine upright, shoulders relaxed  
- Breathe deeply into the **lower belly**  
- Visualize **earth energy** rising up through the feet into the core

Optional: Place hands on tree, rock, or bare ground.

Focus: **Full presence in the body. No mental chatter.**

---

## 🔥 2. Breath + Fire Activation (10 Minutes)

**Purpose**: To awaken Qi and stimulate the feral nervous system.

### Sequence:
- 10 deep belly breaths (inhale 4 sec, exhale 6 sec)
- 3 Rounds of:
  - **Breath of Fire** (30 sec rapid nasal breathing)
  - **Breath Hold** (30 sec retention after final exhale)
  - During hold: squeeze **perineum + core** to direct energy upward

Visualization: A **rising current of fire** moving from pelvis up the spine.

This stage activates:
- Heat  
- Mental clarity  
- Sexual and primal life force

---

## 🐺 3. Feral Movement Practice (10 Minutes)

**Purpose**: To reconnect the body to its **animal intelligence**.

Movements should be done slowly, with control and full breath awareness.

### Core Elements:
- **Crawls** (low, panther-style)
- **Deep Squats** (hips open, heels grounded)
- **Controlled Jumps / Landings**
- **Pushups** (slow, breathing through tension)
- **Spine Rolls & Neck Circles**

No mirrors. No music. Just body, breath, and instinct.

Optional: Practice barefoot and shirtless to increase skin-to-environment feedback.

---

## 🧲 4. Energy Lock-In (5 Minutes)

**Purpose**: To compress, store, and stabilize Qi for use throughout the day.

### Practice:
- Sit cross-legged or stand in Horse Stance  
- Hands over **lower dantian** (3 fingers below navel)  
- Inhale through nose (4 sec)  
- Exhale through nose (6 sec), **squeeze perineum + core** to lock energy

Repeat for 10 cycles.

At the end, say silently or aloud:

> “I hold. I build. I do not spill.  
> My energy is mine to shape.”

---

## 🎯 5. Intent Charge (2–5 Minutes)

**Purpose**: To direct the stored energy into a chosen outcome.

Instructions:
- Choose **one focus**:  
  - A creative act  
  - A performance  
  - A confrontation  
  - A healing  
  - A ritual  
  - A silence

- Visualize that moment or action as if it has already happened  
- Pour the **energy of the practice into it** like charging a sigil

Optional: Speak a verbal command, such as:

> “Today I direct this fire into creation.”  
> “This energy will strike where I choose.”  
> “I walk in presence. I move in power.”
