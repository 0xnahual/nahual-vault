# 🧱 4 Sacred Mobility Portals – *Unlock the Body of the Nahual*

These are the 4 mobility gates you must open to **kick like a god**, **flow like an animal**, and **stand like a mountain**.

---

## 🌀 1. Hip Portal – *The Gate of Power*

**Why:**  
- Controls kicks, stances, takedowns, and animal flow.  
- Most modern bodies are locked here.

**Essential Openers:**  
- 90/90 switches  
- Cossack squats  
- Deep squat holds (Malasana)  
- Frog stretch  
- Tactical frog rocks

**Mastery Test:**  
Hold **Ma Bu (Horse Stance)** at knee height, knees pushed out, for 2 minutes.

---

## 🌪 2. Hamstring Portal – *The Bowstring of the Warrior*

**Why:**  
- Controls reach, balance, and spine freedom.  
- Tight hammies = weak extension, fragile lower back.

**Essential Openers:**  
- Seated forward fold (active)  
- Jefferson curls (light weight, controlled)  
- Elevated hamstring stretch  
- PNF stretching (contract 10s / relax 20s)

**Mastery Test:**  
Touch your toes **cold**, knees straight, spine relaxed.

---

## 🔥 3. Spine + Thoracic Portal – *The Axis of Spirit*

**Why:**  
- Kicks start from the core.  
- Rotation and breath come from a mobile spine.

**Essential Openers:**  
- Cat/Cow + segmental spine waves  
- Thoracic rotation (on all fours)  
- Wall angels  
- Jefferson curl (double impact)  
- Qi Gong spinal spirals

**Mastery Test:**  
Rotate thoracic spine > 45° without moving hips.

---

## 🌊 4. Ankle + Foot Portal – *The Root of the Beast*

**Why:**  
- Controls balance, kicking base, footwork, grounding.  
- A weak root = broken structure.

**Essential Openers:**  
- Ankle dorsiflexion stretch (knee-to-wall)  
- Deep squat hold  
- Toe lifts + calf raises  
- Barefoot walking + balance drills

**Mastery Test:**  
Knee touches wall **4 inches away** with heel flat.

---

## 🧘 Final Integration: *Flow Rituals*

Once these portals are open, integrate with:  
- Animal Flow  
- Capoeira drills  
- Kung Fu / Shadow fighting  
- Qi Gong movement sequences
