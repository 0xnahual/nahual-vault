## PART 1: THE WRITING ROADMAP

### What needs to be written (in order of priority)

#### Priority 1: IXNAH System — Complete the Product (Months 1-3)

This is the thing that makes money. Every empty file is money left on the table.

**Week 1-2: The 7 Base Postures (complete each one)**
For EACH posture (Ma Bu, Gong Bu, Pu Bu, Asian Squat, Dead Hang, Tree Pose, Forward Fold), write:

1. **What it is** — 2-3 sentences. No history, no philosophy. What the body does.
2. **Why it matters** — What breaks in a human who can't do this. What unlocks in one who can. Keep it biological (joints, fascia, nervous system), not spiritual.
3. **The test** — A clear pass/fail. "Can you hold Ma Bu for 2 minutes with knees tracking over toes, weight in heels, spine neutral?" Binary. No "listen to your body" vagueness.
4. **Progression protocol** — Week 1 looks like X. Week 4 looks like Y. Week 8 looks like Z. Exact times, exact sets, exact rest periods.
5. **Common mistakes** — The 3 things people do wrong. With corrections.
6. **What unlocks next** — After mastering this posture, what becomes possible (next posture, next skill, next movement pattern).

Target: ~1,500 words per posture. Total: ~10,500 words. At 500 words/day = 3 weeks.

**Week 3-4: The Nervous System Protocol**
Expand the one-page concept into a trainable system:

1. **Warrior Mode activation drill** — Breathing pattern, body position, mental cue. Step by step. How to enter it in 60 seconds.
2. **Mystic Mode activation drill** — Same format. How to downregulate in 60 seconds.
3. **The Switch drill** — How to go from warrior to mystic and back. Timed protocol. Measurable by heart rate if they have a watch, measurable by breath count if they don't.
4. **Integration with postures** — Which postures train warrior mode (Ma Bu under load, Gong Bu lunges). Which train mystic mode (Forward Fold, Dead Hang). How to sequence them.

Target: ~3,000 words. 1 week.

**Week 5-6: The 9 Stages — Make Them Real**
Each stage gets:
1. **Physical markers** — What your body can do at this stage. Measurable.
2. **Mental markers** — What your mind does at this stage. Observable patterns.
3. **The trap** — What kills people at this stage. The specific way they quit or regress.
4. **The breakthrough** — What gets you to the next stage. One specific action.
5. **Your own experience** — 3-5 sentences of what it was like for YOU at this stage. You lived stages 0-4+. Write from experience, not theory.

Target: ~800 words per stage × 10 = 8,000 words. 2 weeks.

**Week 7-8: The 10 Superpowers — Attach Protocols**
Each superpower currently reads like a Wikipedia entry. Rewrite each one as:
1. **The skill** — One sentence definition.
2. **The 4-week protocol** — Exact daily practice to begin developing it.
3. **The test** — How you know you have it.
4. **Prerequisite** — Which IXNAH posture(s) and which stage you should be at before attempting.

Target: ~600 words per superpower × 10 = 6,000 words. 2 weeks.

**Week 9-10: The 9 Pillars — Connect to Body**
