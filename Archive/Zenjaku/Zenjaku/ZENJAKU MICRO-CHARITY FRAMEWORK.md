# 🌱 ZENJAKU MICRO-CHARITY FRAMEWORK

## 1. CORE PRINCIPLE
Help the helpers.  
Zenjaku supports micro-charities: small, underfunded, high-impact operations run by real people who need immediate help.  
Direct, immediate impact with zero bureaucracy.

---

## 2. WHY MICRO-CHARITIES?
10× more impact per dollar.  
No corporate overhead.  
No bureaucracy or delay.  
Money instantly improves real livezs.  
Collectors feel real emotional connection.  

Examples include:  
• A dog rescue with 200+ animals  
• Local shelters in debt  
• Small community kitchens  
• Grassroots rescuers with no funding  

---

## 3. HOW ZENJAKU HELPS
Burns reduce supply and increase royalty momentum.  
50% of royalties are sent directly to micro-charities.  
1/1 auctions can amplify donation amounts.  
Every collector action creates real-world help.

---

## 4. THE FEEDBACK LOOP
Collectors burn → supply drops → scarcity grows → royalties increase → micro-charities receive funds → visible impact → more engagement → more burns → stronger cycle.  
A positive-sum loop built on meaning and action.

---

## 5. WHY THIS IS GROUNDED
Does not rely on hype or virality.  
Works even at low volume.  
Small donations still create massive impact at micro-charities.  
Ethical, sustainable, long-term design.  
Perfect alignment with Zenjaku’s purpose and narrative.

---
