## **PILAR DE FLUIR — EL DANZANTE DEL CAOS**

  

### **The Shadow**

  

At first, he believes _flow_ means **freedom from all form**.

He rejects discipline, plans, even identity.

He lives only by instinct—changing paths, partners, moods, dreams.

People call him gifted: everything he touches _works_, but nothing lasts.

He becomes the river that never rests long enough to see its reflection.

  

His motto is: _“If I stop, I die.”_

He thinks that’s liberation.

But deep down, it’s fear—fear of stillness, fear of commitment, fear of self.

  

So the shadow of Flow is **drift.**

He becomes the opposite of presence: a ghost carried by every current.
