# **🥋 Calisthenics Skills for Martial Arts**

  

## **🔑 Core Skill Work**

  

### **L-Sit / V-Sit**

- ✅ Builds hip flexor + core compression strength
    
- ✅ Improves active flexibility (leg lift under control)
    
- 🎯 **Transfer:** higher front kicks, sharper roundhouse chamber, stronger teeps
    

---

### **Front Lever (Tuck → Straddle → Full)**

- ✅ Lat + scapula power
    
- ✅ Teaches explosive pulling/retraction
    
- 🎯 **Transfer:** faster punch retraction, stronger clinch/takedown control
    

---

### **Dragon Flags / Hollow Body Holds**

- ✅ Midsection anti-extension strength
    
- ✅ Spinal armor for rotation + impact absorption
    
- 🎯 **Transfer:** harder hooks/roundhouses, stronger core against body shots
    

---

### **Handstand (Wall → Freestanding → Press)**

- ✅ Shoulder stability + balance under load
    
- ✅ Builds whole-body tension awareness
    
- 🎯 **Transfer:** better guard, posture, rooted stance, balance in kicks
    

---

### **Pistol Squats / Shrimp Squats**

- ✅ Single-leg strength + stability
    
- ✅ Teaches balance under fatigue
    
- 🎯 **Transfer:** stable high kicks, spinning kicks, agile footwork
    

---

### **Back Lever**

- ✅ Posterior chain + scapula stability
    
- ✅ Shoulder + spine control in extension
    
- 🎯 **Transfer:** resistance in sprawls, better posture in grappling
    

---

### **Explosive Skills (Clap Push-ups, Muscle-up Variants)**

- ✅ Develops fast-twitch power
    
- ✅ Improves explosive pressing/pulling
    
- 🎯 **Transfer:** punch snap, spring in flying knees/kicks, explosive takedowns
    

---

## **⚔️ Tier List for Martial Arts**

- **Must-Have**: L-Sit, V-Sit, Front Lever, Dragon Flags, Pistol Squats
    
- **Nice-to-Have**: Handstands, Back Lever, Explosive Push Variations
    
- **Show Only**: Planche, Human Flag (badass but low fight carryover)
    

---

👉 In short: **skills that compress (L-sit, V-sit, dragon flag) or stabilize one leg (pistol)** = highest kick benefit.

**Skills that pull/retract (front lever)** = sharper strikes + grappling.

**Skills that balance/stabilize (handstands, back lever)** = posture and flow.
