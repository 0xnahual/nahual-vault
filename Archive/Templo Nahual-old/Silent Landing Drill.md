## **Silent Landing Drill (The Nahual Fall)**

**Purpose:** Build landing control, absorb force like an animal, protect the knee.

**Equipment:** 1 low plyo box (20–40 cm)

---

### **Instructions**

1. Step to the edge of the box.
2. **Step off** — do **not** jump or push.
3. As you fall, let gravity take you.
4. **Land as silently as possible.**  
   - Knees track over toes (no collapse inward).  
   - Hips hinge back slightly to absorb force.  
   - Core engaged.
5. Hold the landing **perfectly still** for **3 seconds.**
6. Stand up slow.
7. **Rest 10–20 seconds** between reps.

---

### **Sets / Reps**
- **5 reps total**
- **1× per day**

Do **not** increase height until the landing is **silent and stable**.

---

### **Success Markers**
- No sound on impact.
- No knee wobble.
- You feel the **hips** take the load, not the knees.
- Landing feels **springy**, not heavy.

---

### **Common Mistakes**
- Jumping off instead of stepping.
- Landing loud.
- Knees collapsing inward.
- Rushing the hold.

---

### **Key Cue**
> *“Land like a cat, not like a human.”*
