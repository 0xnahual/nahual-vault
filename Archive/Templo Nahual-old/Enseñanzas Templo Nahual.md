# **TEMPLO NAHUAL**

  

## **Content & Teaching Guide (v1)**

  

### **Core Principle**

  

**The body leads.**

**Biology explains.**

**Mysticism is never named.**

---

## **1. WHAT YOU ARE ALLOWED TO TEACH**

  

You **are allowed** to teach:

  

### **✅ Body mechanics**

- posture
    
- joint alignment
    
- muscle engagement
    
- leverage
    
- balance
    
- tension vs relaxation
    

  

### **✅ Biology & nervous system (basic)**

- why shaking happens
    
- why breath matters
    
- why posture collapses
    
- why fatigue changes form
    

  

### **✅ Routines**

- sets
    
- holds
    
- time
    
- progressions
    
- regressions
    

  

You are **not** teaching enlightenment.

You are teaching **how the body organizes under load**.

---

## **2. WHAT YOU NEVER TEACH OR SAY**

  

Hard bans:

- ❌ “awakens”
    
- ❌ “unlocks”
    
- ❌ “energy channels”
    
- ❌ “ancient wisdom”
    
- ❌ “this will change your life”
    
- ❌ “spiritually”
    

  

If it sounds like a podcast quote, delete it.

---

## **3. THE VOICE & ROLE YOU PLAY**

  

You are a **martial arts instructor**, not a guru.

  

### **How you speak:**

- short
    
- factual
    
- corrective
    
- neutral
    

  

### **Acceptable tone:**

  

> “When the stance goes lower, the pelvis has to stay neutral or the spine collapses.”

  

### **Unacceptable tone:**

  

> “This stance teaches humility and inner strength.”

  

Even if true — **don’t say it**.

---

## **4. CONTENT FORMAT (NON-NEGOTIABLE)**

  

### **A. CAMERA RULES**

- Never speak to the camera
    
- No “hey guys”
    
- No addressing the viewer
    
- Camera observes like CCTV
    

---

### **B. AUDIO STRUCTURE**

  

**Option 1 – Silent**

- ambient sound
    
- music
    
- breath
    

  

**Option 2 – Voice-over**

- AFTER recording
    
- observational
    
- instructional
    

  

Never live-talking while training.

---

## **5. HOW YOU EXPLAIN (THE FORMULA)**

  

All explanations follow this formula:

  

**Action → Body Response → Mechanical Result**

  

### **Example (Ma Bu):**

  

> “Lowering Ma Bu increases demand on the hips.

> If the hips disengage, the knees collapse.

> When the hips stay active, the spine stacks naturally.”

  

That’s it.

No meaning. No promise.

---

## **6. ROUTINES (YOU ARE ALLOWED – HERE’S HOW)**

  

You **can and should** post routines.

  

### **Routine Rules**

- Simple
    
- Repeatable
    
- No hype names
    
- No mystical framing
    

  

### **Example Routine (acceptable)**

  

**Ma Bu Routine**

- 3 rounds
    
- 60–90s hold
    
- Rest until breath normalizes
    
- Focus: pelvis neutral, knees open, breath steady
    

  

Optional VO:

  

> “Most posture failure comes from the hips disengaging before the legs fatigue.”

---

### **Bad Routine (don’t do this)**

  

❌ “IXNAH Root Awakening Protocol”

❌ “This routine builds warrior spirit”

  

No spells. No branding inside the routine.

---

## **7. CORRECTION VOCABULARY (LIMITED ON PURPOSE)**

  

Your **spoken words during movement** are limited to:

- Lower.
    
- Relax.
    
- Root.
    
- Stack.
    
- Breathe.
    
- Again.
    

  

That’s it.

  

Anything else is ego leaking.

---

## **8. CONTENT PILLARS (ROTATE, DON’T MIX)**

  

Each video focuses on **ONE**:

1. **STANCE**
    
    Ma Bu, Gong Bu, Pu Bu
    
2. **FRAME**
    
    Pull-ups, hangs, push variations
    
3. **CONTACT**
    
    Clinch, balance, throw entry
    
4. **FLOW**
    
    Tricking, transitions, recovery
    

  

Never mix pillars in one clip.

---

## **9. CAPTION RULE (CRITICAL)**

  

Captions are **technical notes**, not marketing.

  

### **Good captions:**

- “Ma Bu – pelvis alignment under fatigue.”
    
- “Posture breaks when breath becomes shallow.”
    
- “Hips stabilize the spine, not the legs.”
    

  

### **Bad captions:**

- “Train like a warrior”
    
- “Unlock your potential”
    
- “Follow for transformation”
    

  

If the caption could be on a **whiteboard in a gym**, it’s correct.

---

## **10. WHY THIS WORKS (AND YOU DON’T EXPLAIN THIS)**

  

You never say this publicly, but internally understand it:

- Long holds quiet the mind
    
- Breath regulates the nervous system
    
- Alignment creates efficiency
    
- Efficiency feels like “energy”
    

  

People will feel it **without you naming it**.

  

That’s the whole game.
