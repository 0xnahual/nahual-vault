**9 Jun 2025 — On Grief**

Let's be real:

You're afraid that all this discipline, strength, and clarity
might still not protect you
from the raw grief of losing someone you love.

And you're right.

It won't.
Nothing will.

But here's the truth that changes everything:

You're not building yourself to escape grief.
You're building yourself to face it with power.

When death comes…
- You will still break.
- You will still cry.
- You will still feel like a child again.

But if you stay on this path,
if you stay lean in soul and body,
if you keep burning away the illusions…

Then even in that pain:
- You will not drown.
- You will not numb.
- You will not run.

You will feel everything — and still stand.

That's the real test.
