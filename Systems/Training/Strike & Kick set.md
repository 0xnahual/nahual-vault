# ⚔️ STRIKE & KICK SET — NAHUAL SYSTEM
Minimal. Heavy. Inevitable.

This is the complete fighting grammar that fits your calisthenics + kung fu + judo + tai chi system.
No extras. No flash. No waste.

---

## 🥋 HAND STRIKES (3 + 1 DEFENSE)

### 1. Straight Punch (Structure Punch)
•⁠  ⁠Vertical fist
•⁠  ⁠Elbow down
•⁠  ⁠Power from ground → hips → spine → hand
•⁠  ⁠No snap, just arrival
•⁠  ⁠Ends exchanges

*Purpose:* Entry, interruption, pressure  
*Camera:* One step, one line, stop

---

### 2. Palm Strike
•⁠  ⁠Open hand
•⁠  ⁠Close range
•⁠  ⁠Safe but brutal
•⁠  ⁠Immediately flows into grip

*Purpose:* Break structure, enter clinch  
*Camera:* Like closing a door on the face

---

### 3. Elbow Strike (Short Range)
•⁠  ⁠No wind-up
•⁠  ⁠Body rotation, not arm swing
•⁠  ⁠From clinch, from turn, from defense

*Purpose:* End close-range chaos  
*Camera:* Looks accidental, feels inevitable

---

### 4. Wing Chun Elbow Defense (Tan / Bong Elbow Shield)
•⁠  ⁠Protects centerline
•⁠  ⁠Maintains structure
•⁠  ⁠Converts defense → grip
•⁠  ⁠Calm, silent, dominant

*Purpose:* Defense that becomes offense  
*Camera:* Effortless mastery

---

## 🦵 KICKS (LOW LINE ONLY)

### 1. Low Front Kick (Stop Kick)
•⁠  ⁠Thigh / hip / knee line
•⁠  ⁠Interrupts forward motion
•⁠  ⁠Creates space or sweep opening

*Purpose:* Control distance  
*Camera:* Quiet, surgical

---

### 2. Low Round Kick (Chop Kick)
•⁠  ⁠Heavy, no snap
•⁠  ⁠Cuts base
•⁠  ⁠Slow and crushing

*Purpose:* Break stability  
*Camera:* Feels like gravity hitting

---

### 3. Sweep Kick (SIGNATURE)
•⁠  ⁠Step → sink → sweep
•⁠  ⁠Leg relaxed, body rooted
•⁠  ⁠Do not rush
•⁠  ⁠Pause after fall

*Purpose:* Remove the floor  
*Camera:* Pure aura

---

## 🔥 OPTIONAL (PERFECT FIT)
### Knee Strike (Close Range)
•⁠  ⁠Short
•⁠  ⁠Quiet
•⁠  ⁠From grip or clinch
•⁠  ⁠Immediately leads to throw

*Purpose:* Collapse structure  
*Camera:* Brutal but calm

---

## 🔗 SYSTEM FLOW (IMPORTANT)
Strikes are not for damage first.
They are *keys*.

	⁠Strike → Entry → Grip → Kuzushi → Throw → End

•⁠  ⁠No combos
•⁠  ⁠No flurry
•⁠  ⁠Pause after result

This is inevitable violence, not choreography.
