# THE WARRIOR CEREMONY: A Rite of Fire and Will
_1 Jul 2025_

## Overview
A ceremonial initiation designed to awaken the inner warrior through sweat, stillness, movement, and psychedelic presence.
Rooted in ancient principles, guided by primal music, and centered around fire.

---

## Core Intent

> "This is not a trip. This is a rite."
> "You are not here to heal what is broken.
> You are here to burn what is false."

---

## PHASE 0 – IGNITION RITE (0–20 min)

### Fire & Intention
- Each participant receives a **piece of wood**.
- Ceremony leader speaks:

  > "This wood is your weakness.
  > Your excuses.
  > Your addiction to comfort.
  > Your fear of your own power."

- One by one, participants **place their wood into the fire** in silence.
- Group takes **1g of psilocybin mushrooms** immediately after burning.
- Sit barefoot in silence, facing the fire.
  Eyes open. Breath deep.
  Wait.

---

## PHASE 1 – BODY AWAKENING (20–40 min)

- **3 Rounds:**
  - 10 slow pushups
  - 15 deep squats
  - 30 seconds Ma Bu (Horse Stance)
  - 1 minute stillness by the fire

- Music: Deep ambient tribal drums, subtle visionary tones
- Purpose: Sync breath and body, warm up system, activate presence

---

## PHASE 2 – TRIAL BY FIRE (40–65 min)

### Ritual Circuit (Repeat 2–3x):
- Ma Bu – 2 minutes
- Gong Bu (Bow Stance) – 45 seconds each side
- 20 Pushups (every 5th rep = breath + sound)
- 10 Burpees
- 1-Minute Plank
- **LIVE visionary rock / tribal drums** at peak intensity

> Leader speaks during holds:
>
> "Let the beast tremble.
> Not your will."

---

## PHASE 3 – COLLAPSE & MESSAGE (65–80 min)

- Lie down. Full surrender.
- Silence. No music.
- Eyes closed. Hands on the earth.

> Ask inward:
> - "What did I burn?"
> - "What remains?"

- Journal or sit in wordless awareness.

---

## PHASE 4 – RECLAMATION (80–90 min)

- Sit back up, facing the fire
- Cold water available
- 3 breath cycles: inhale nose, exhale sound
- Grounding in silence

> Final closing words from the guide:
>
> "You didn't come here to be saved.
> You came here to remember.
> You are the fire. Now walk like it."

---

## PHASE 5 – THE RETURN (90–105 min)

### Stretching + Grounding (10 min)
- Seated or lying circle around the fire
- Intuitive full-body stretches
- Leader speaks softly:
  > "You've broken patterns in the body.
  > Now soften them with grace."

### Flute & Meditation (5–10 min)
- **Live native-style flute playing**
- Eyes closed. Hands on heart or earth.
- Deep, wordless meditation.

> "You met the flame.
> You met the beast.
> You stood.
> Now return as the one who remembers."

- Optional: closing hum / mantra / group silence

---

## Ceremony Leader Notes

- **Leader doses 1g** with the group to match frequency
- Must be physically and mentally prepared to lead and endure all movements
- Must be trained in calm presence and verbal command
- A sober **guardian/helper** must be present for emergencies
- No ego. No performance. Only presence + power

---

## Ceremony Principles

- Fire is the center. It is the mirror and destroyer.
- Mushrooms amplify, but do not define.
- Physical suffering awakens spiritual will.
- Music transmits archetypes.
- Stillness is where the wisdom lands.
