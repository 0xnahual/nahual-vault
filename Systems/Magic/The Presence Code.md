# THE PRESENCE CODE
_(For Life — not just the Temple)_
_11 Aug 2025_

---

## The 10 Laws

### 1. No Ghost Movements
Every step, every gesture, every rep is deliberate.
No autopilot. No sloppy body language. Move like your actions leave ripples in eternity — because they do.

### 2. Eyes Like Blades
When you speak to someone, look at them. When you train, see what you're doing. Your gaze is your anchor — lose it, and your mind follows.

### 3. Single-Point Focus
Do one thing at a time. No split attention. No phone in hand while listening. No music when someone's speaking. You either give something your whole mind or you give it nothing.

### 4. Guard the Breath
Before reacting, breathe. Long. Deep. Controlled.
Presence starts in oxygen, not in thought. Never let the breath be hijacked by fear, anger, or rush.

### 5. Anchor to the Senses
Hear the smallest sound. Smell the air. Feel the ground under your feet. Taste your food instead of inhaling it. The senses are the gates to the Now — keep them open.

### 6. Kill Background Noise
Don't keep things running "just because" — music, videos, chatter. If it's not feeding your current action, it's stealing presence.

### 7. Speak from the Flame
Words are spells. If you can't speak from truth, don't speak. Don't fill air with noise to avoid silence — let silence be the default.

### 8. Stand Like a Pillar
Whether waiting in line or speaking on stage, own your space. Shoulders down, spine long, weight balanced. Presence starts in posture before it hits the mind.

### 9. Ritual Before Entry
Before entering any space (room, dojo, meeting, home), take one breath and choose how you enter. Never stumble in mindlessly — arrive.

### 10. End As Strong As You Begin
Leave with the same presence you entered with. Don't fade out. Close conversations. Put things away clean. Step out like you meant to be there.

## PRESENCE VIOLATIONS LIST
_(Kill these instantly)_

### 1. Eyes on the Phone While Walking
Zombie posture. Head down, breath shallow. You are prey in this state.
**Kill it:** Pocket the phone. Lift your head. Own the ground.

### 2. Fidgeting Without Awareness
Tapping fingers, scratching, bouncing legs — energy leaks.
**Kill it:** Still the body. Feel the urge. Breathe through it.

### 3. Rushed, Shallow Breathing
The breath has gone high and tight. You're in survival mode without realizing it.
**Kill it:** One deep exhale, then slow inhale. Reset the pace.

### 4. Half-Listening
Nodding while your mind is somewhere else. You're not here.
**Kill it:** Lock eyes. Repeat their last words in your head. Anchor back.

### 5. Dead Gaze
Staring at nothing, unfocused eyes. The mind is drifting into fog.
**Kill it:** Pick one object. See it in detail. Feel your body where it is.

### 6. Multitasking in Sacred Moments
Checking messages while eating, training, or speaking with someone.
**Kill it:** Drop the extra task. Honor the moment in front of you.

### 7. Sloppy Posture
Shoulders rolled, spine bent, weight dumped on one leg. Presence leaks from the frame first.
**Kill it:** Reset spine. Drop shoulders. Balance weight. Open chest.

### 8. Speech Filler
"Uh," "like," "you know" — filling silence because you're afraid of it.
**Kill it:** Embrace the pause. Speak only when ready.

### 9. Autopilot Hands
Picking at clothes, tapping objects, scrolling without intent.
**Kill it:** Freeze hands. Make them do something on purpose.

### 10. Exit Without Closure
Leaving a space or conversation with half your mind still there.
**Kill it:** Take one breath, acknowledge you are leaving, and walk out fully present.
