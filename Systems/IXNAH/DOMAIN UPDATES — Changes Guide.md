## Summary of Changes

### 1. VIOLENCE → [NEW NAME] (Pending: PRIMAL, ANIMAL, or other)

The domain formerly called VIOLENCE is renamed. Its meaning changes from "offensive combat against humans" to "the animal body — primal physical capabilities reclaimed from domestication."

**New domain contains:** Climbing, brachiating, crawling, gorilla grip, sprinting (predator), hanging, dragging, explosive jumping, swimming, throwing objects, raw unstructured physical power.

**What moved OUT:** All combat application (striking, throwing, grappling techniques) moved to DEFENDING.

### 2. DEFENDING absorbs all combat

DEFENDING now contains all combat application, including Judo throws, clinch control, ground survival, and breakfalls. The philosophy: the IXNAH warrior does not attack. All combat is defensive — neutralization, control, protection.

**No striking in IXNAH.** No boxing. No kicking offensively. Combat = Judo + BJJ (future). The warrior closes distance with animal speed (PRIMAL domain), clinches with gorilla grip (PRIMAL domain), and throws/controls with technique (DEFENDING domain).

### 3. K1 removed from the system

All references to K1/kickboxing training should be removed. The combat art is Judo (3x/week), with BJJ as a future addition.
