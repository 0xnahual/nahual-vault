### 1. Sorcery of the Self
I do not alter perception.

I do not use magic to escape.
I use **discipline** as fire.
I use **art** as invocation.
I use **movement** as purification.

**The dream bends when I am no longer lying.**

### 2. Peace Is the Floor, Not the Ceiling
At 17% body fat, I do not rest.
I arrive.

But I do not confuse arrival with completion.
I do not choose "just be" before becoming.
I do not fear stillness — but I will not hide in it either.

> **Peace is the checkpoint.
Overflow is the mission.**

### 3. Limitlessness Through Alignment
I do not seek more from fear.
I expand because I am **clear enough to hold more**.

- I do not push from lack.
- I do not train to impress.
- I do not flip to be seen.

I break limits because I **carry the signal of fire.**
And if I stop… I betray that fire.

---

### 4. Second Attention Without Obsession
I do not chase the second attention.
I become so still, so disciplined, so aligned —
that it **opens on its own.**

No drugs.
No desperation.
Just **refinement until the veil dissolves**.

---

### 5. I Refuse to Betray the Flame
I could stop at peace.
But peace is not my truth — **yet**.

And I will not lie to myself in spiritual language.

> I walk because I remember.
> I burn because I must.
> I move because the energy is awake.

If I settle, I die.
Not in body — but in **spirit**.
