## Part 1: How Long — The Math

[[Coaching — Final Training Schedule|La Forja]] isn't a calendar phase. It's a conditions-based phase. It ends when specific things are true, not when a date arrives.

### The Body Recomp Timeline

**Current state:** 90 kg, ~21% body fat
**Target:** 90 kg, 14.5% body fat

That means:
- Current fat mass: ~18.9 kg
- Target fat mass: ~13.05 kg
- **Fat to lose: ~5.85 kg**
- Current lean mass: ~71.1 kg
- Target lean mass: ~76.95 kg
- **Muscle to gain: ~5.85 kg**

You need to lose ~6 kg of fat and gain ~6 kg of muscle at the same body weight. Simultaneous recomp.

**Realistic rates at your training age and level:**
- Natural muscle gain (intermediate lifter, 2+ years training): 0.5–1.0 kg/month with perfect nutrition
- Fat loss during recomp (not a cut, eating at maintenance or slight surplus): 0.5–1.0 kg/month
- Your recent rate: +2.9 kg muscle in one month — this is peak performance and won't sustain. That was beginner/intermediate recomp magic meeting perfect conditions. Expect it to slow.
- Sustainable average: ~0.75 kg muscle gain / 0.75 kg fat loss per month

**At 0.75 kg/month recomp rate: ~8 months.** Best case.
**At 0.5 kg/month (more realistic long-term): ~12 months.**
**With the 40%→70% food logging transition, adjustment period, potential stalls: 12-18 months.**

### The Honest Timeline

| Scenario | Duration | End Date | Probability |
|----------|----------|----------|-------------|
| Everything perfect (nutrition 70%+, no injuries, no stalls) | 8-10 months | Nov 2026 – Jan 2027 | 15% |
| Realistic (some stalls, nutrition improves gradually, minor setbacks) | 12-15 months | Mar – Jun 2027 | 60% |
| Conservative (injuries, life disruptions, slow food logging adoption) | 16-20 months | Jul – Nov 2027 | 25% |

**Working estimate: La Forja runs March 2026 → March 2027.** One year. With a 3-month buffer to June 2027 if things slow down.

### Why Not Shorter

You cannot rush recomp. You're not cutting — cutting would be faster (3-4 months) but you'd lose muscle. The entire point of recomping at 90 kg is to arrive at 14.5% body fat with MORE muscle, not less. This requires patience and precision.

The +2.9 kg muscle month proved your body can grow. But sustaining that rate over 8+ months is biochemically impossible without PEDs. The body adapts. Expect the rate to roughly halve after the first 3-4 months at the new training volume.

### Why Not Longer

If La Forja takes more than 18 months, something is broken:
- Food logging never hit 70% → nutrition is the bottleneck
- Injuries accumulated → volume was too high
- PRs stalled for months → programming needs changing
- Creative blocks were never protected → the phase lost its purpose

The trigger system in the [[Coaching — Training & Phase Analysis]] document handles all of these. La Forja has built-in self-correction. If it's dragging past 18 months, the phase didn't work — you reassess everything.

## Part 3: What Comes After — La Revelación

### Why "La Revelación"

La Forja is private. It's the fire, the discipline, the daily grind that nobody sees. You're building in silence.

La Revelación is when the world sees what you built. The reveal. The output phase.

If La Forja is the forge, La Revelación is pulling the blade out of the fire and showing it to the world.

### When It Starts

La Revelación begins when **three of these five conditions are true:**

| Condition | Metric |
|-----------|--------|
| Body fat at or below 16% | Pliegues measurement confirmed |
| Emergency fund at 3+ months | Bank balance verified |
| First creative work shipped | Song, chapter, or app build released publicly |
| @0xnahual has posted consistently for 4+ weeks | Social media presence alive |
| Studio is functional | GST: Arreglar Estudio marked Done |

You don't need all five. But you need three. That's when the foundation is strong enough to build on publicly.

**Estimated start: Q4 2026 or Q1 2027.**

### What La Revelación Looks Like

| Domain | La Forja (Now) | La Revelación (Next) |
|--------|---------------|---------------------|
| **Body** | Recomp in silence. Log. Train. Measure. | The transformation goes public. Before/after content. The 127→90→14.5% story told. Video drops. The image becomes the brand. |
| **[[What is IXNAH\|IXNAH]]** | Build the system. Train the domains. Fill the book outline. | Start teaching. First IXNAH content published — videos, essays, demonstrations. The system goes from private practice to public framework. |
| **Music** | Learn Ableton. Produce demos. Practice daily. | Release. Ixtlan Project singles drop. NΛHUΛL folk recordings go live. The music exists in the world. |
| **[[Kung Fu Temple App Spec\|El Templo]]** | Build MVP. Use it yourself. Export data. | Beta release. Other people use it. Feedback loop begins. The app becomes a product, not just a personal tool. |
| **Book** | Outline. Draft chapters. Test ideas in practice. | Write seriously. First chapters or essays published. The IXNAH philosophy exists as text others can read. |
| **Brand** | Architecture defined. Handle claimed. Content: zero. | @0xnahual becomes active. The content machine turns on. Documentary-style content. Training clips. Music previews. Philosophical posts. |
| **Financial** | Save. Build emergency fund. Track expenses. | Invest in creative infrastructure. Maybe reduce hours at day job if creative income grows. Or negotiate remote flexibility for travel/projects. |

### The Schedule Shift in La Revelación

The training schedule doesn't change much. What changes is the **creative block content.**

In La Forja, creative blocks are about learning and building:
- Learning Ableton
- Building the app
- Drafting the book
- Setting up the studio

In La Revelación, creative blocks shift to **shipping and publishing:**
- Finishing and releasing tracks
- Editing and posting content
- Writing and publishing chapters
- Recording and sharing IXNAH demonstrations

Same time blocks. Different output. The discipline stays. The direction shifts from inward to outward.

**What will you be turned into?** A 90 kg, 14.5% body fat martial artist with documented PRs across 6 domains, real Judo and K1 experience, a functional studio, financial security, and — for the first time — creative work that exists in the world instead of just in your vault.

## Part 5: The One Thing That Determines Everything

La Forja's success is not determined by how hard you train. You already train hard. It's not determined by how sophisticated your systems are. Your systems are already sophisticated.

**[[Coaching — Final Training Schedule|La Forja]]'s success is determined by whether you ship.**

If you reach March 2027 at 14.5% body fat with zero songs released, zero chapters published, zero app users, and zero social media presence — La Forja failed. The body is ready but the output isn't. You'd be the strongest, leanest guy nobody ever heard of.

The physical transformation is the engine. The creative output is the vehicle. The engine means nothing without the vehicle.

Every month of La Forja, ask yourself: **"Did I ship something this month?"**

Not "did I practice." Not "did I plan." Not "did I work on it."

**Did I ship.**

That's the only question that matters.
