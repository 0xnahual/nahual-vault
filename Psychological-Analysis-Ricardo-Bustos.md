### Based on the complete 0xnahual vault (200+ writings, journals, creative works, and strategy documents)

## I. THE CORE ARCHITECTURE OF THE PSYCHE

Ricardo's inner world is organized around a **tripartite self-model** that he developed through DMT ceremonies, Jungian active imagination, and raw introspection. He calls them:

1. **El Espiritu (Neza / th0ndrsoul)** — The higher self. Source of infinite creativity, connection to the divine, purpose-driven action, presence. When dominant: prolific writing, musical flow, discipline, spiritual clarity, emotional detachment. Cost: alienation from humanity, loneliness, risk of psychosis (he explicitly names a depressive psychosis episode from over-inhabiting this state).

2. **La Bestia (Sukuna)** — The shadow/animal self. Source of aggression, sexuality, ambition, survival instinct, connection to the earthly and human. When dominant: pursuit of money/fame/sex, physical strength, social charisma, ability to understand and connect with ordinary people. Cost: addiction spirals (alcohol, masturbation, trading), loss of spiritual connection, feeling abandoned by God.

3. **Ricardo (the conscious ego)** — The mediator trying to negotiate between these two forces. His entire vault is essentially the record of this negotiation.

This is not metaphor to him. He writes dialogues between these parts. He invokes them by name. He makes contracts between them ("Espiritu and Bestia, I need you to integrate for the next 3 months"). This is Jungian active imagination practiced with complete sincerity and at times frightening depth — the "Awakening the Soul" automatic writing session is a real-time descent into the unconscious, spelling errors and all.

**Key insight:** Ricardo's central psychological project is **integration**, not transcendence. He has repeatedly discovered that trying to live purely as the Espiritu leads to isolation and psychosis, while surrendering to the Bestia leads to addiction and purposelessness. His most mature writing acknowledges that both are needed, that the "balance" is not 50/50 but a dynamic, context-dependent dance. This mirrors the individuation process Jung described almost exactly.

## II. THE ADDICTION PATTERN

Ricardo documents four interlocking addictions:

1. **Alcohol** — The most physically destructive. He records losing 3 gym days after a single binge, vomiting 4 times at a party, entering spirals where "I can't stop once I start." His January-March 2024 experiment (zero alcohol) correlates with his highest-functioning period. He has quit and relapsed multiple times. As of the most recent writings, he appears to have quit successfully — the Vision.md document lists addictions as "gone."

2. **Masturbation/Pornography** — He frames this through the lens of "ki spill" (energy loss). His records show a pattern: 8+ days of retention produce clarity, confidence, and creative power. A single relapse triggers a binge (5-6 times in a day), followed by physical illness (headaches, flu symptoms), shame, and loss of motivation. He has developed his own theory that semen retention works not through hormones but through psychological self-mastery — "knowing you can dominate your mind gives you a boost in spiritual self-esteem that lets you surpass limits." This is more sophisticated than the typical nofap ideology; it locates the power in the discipline, not in mystical energy.

3. **Trading/Crypto** — Perhaps the most insidious because it masquerades as a skill. He was genuinely successful (made millions by 25), which makes it harder to quit. He records losing $400 on a short, then $1,000 the next day, all while knowing he should stop. He writes "Deja el trading ya" repeatedly across different files, different months. The pattern: anxiety or boredom triggers the urge, trading provides the dopamine hit of potential financial gain, losses create shame, shame triggers the other addictions (alcohol, masturbation), which creates a compound spiral. This is the classic cross-addiction cascade.

4. **DMT/Psychedelics** — The most complicated. DMT is simultaneously his addiction and his medicine. His most profound spiritual experiences came through "xanga" (changa) ceremonies. His entire philosophical framework originated in DMT visions. But he also lists psychedelics among the addictions he has overcome in the Vision document. The resolution appears to be: DMT as intentional ceremony = sacred; DMT as escape or habit = addiction. He seems to have drawn this line.

**The master pattern:** All four addictions serve the same function — they are **shortcuts to states he can access naturally but finds difficult to sustain**. Alcohol shortcutting social connection and pleasure. Masturbation shortcutting sexual energy and connection. Trading shortcutting financial abundance. DMT shortcutting spiritual experience. The entire IXNAH system (postures, breathwork, training) is essentially his attempt to build sustainable, non-addictive pathways to these same states.

## III. THE FAME/ANONYMITY CONFLICT

This is one of the most clearly documented internal conflicts in the vault. It spans dozens of files and never fully resolves. The structure:

**The Espiritu's position:** Anonymity. Mystery. Let the work speak. "Freddy Mercury wouldn't post reels of himself singing or dumb tweets about what he thinks." The power is in the art, not the person. Being famous would be contradictory to the message of inner empowerment. He saw what mild fame did to him in the crypto world — people attacked him online, and he never recovered from the trauma of public cancellation.

**La Bestia's position:** Fame. Status. Recognition. The Bestia wants to be "mamadisimo" (jacked) so people respect him on sight. The Bestia wants a partner, and believes status is required to attract someone at his level. The Bestia wants to be the center of attention, to be recognized, to matter in the external world.

**The wound underneath:** The crypto cancellation. He was a top-5 crypto influencer. A project failed. He lost money, employees, his best friend. People talked about him online without context. This trauma creates a phobic response to public visibility — every time he considers posting content or showing his face, the fear of re-experiencing public humiliation paralyzes him.

**The resolution (as of the latest documents):** One identity. Ricardo Bustos. Faceless content. Body-only training footage. The engineer who never appears on camera. This appears to be a genuine integration — not avoidance of fame, but a redefinition of it. He doesn't need to be recognized on the street. He needs his work to be known. The app, the books, the system — these carry the message. He is the architect, not the billboard.

- Uncertainty about which attraction is "primary"
- A desire for children (which creates internal conflict with his trans attraction)

His framework: the ideal partner must (1) activate the Bestia sexually, (2) share his level of consciousness, and (3) have her own purpose. He recognizes this is a high bar but refuses to compromise.

His relationship to sexuality is also tied to his creative energy theory — sexual release depletes the energy needed for creation, while retention amplifies it. This creates a cycle: long retention periods produce powerful creative output, but the accumulated sexual tension eventually triggers a binge, which triggers shame, which triggers the other addictions.

## V. THE GOD RELATIONSHIP

Ricardo's relationship with the divine is the most consistent thread across the entire vault. It evolves through stages:

1. **Discovery** — Through DMT. The initial vision that "cracked everything open." He experiences God not as a concept but as an overwhelming presence. This is the moment of the shamanic "call."

2. **Surrender** — "Hazme instrumento de tu grandeza" (Make me an instrument of your greatness). This phrase appears across multiple files, multiple months. It's his core prayer. He genuinely believes God gave him his abilities for a purpose and will provide what he needs if he follows that purpose.

3. **Testing** — He records periods of feeling abandoned ("those thoughts that He abandoned me are banal"). The disconnect always correlates with addiction relapse. When he drinks, trades, or binges on masturbation, he feels cut off from God. When he's disciplined, he feels divinely guided. This creates a feedback loop where the divine relationship becomes a motivational structure.

4. **Maturation** — In later writings, God shifts from external entity to internal presence. "God is inside of you." The Espiritu IS the divine within. This is the Nahual philosophy's core — divinity is not above you, it IS you, but only accessible when the ego, mind, and body are aligned.

5. **The exchange principle** — He records a vision where God offered him a trade: lose 20kg of fat in exchange for making his projects popular and receiving more creativity. Whether literal or metaphorical, this reveals his deep belief that spiritual discipline and worldly success are causally linked — not through "manifestation" magic, but through the practical reality that a disciplined body creates a disciplined mind creates disciplined output.

## X. PSYCHOLOGICAL RISKS

1. **Grandiosity cycles** — When the Espiritu is dominant, the writing becomes messianic: "I will be one of the greatest artists in history," "I have infinite power." When the Bestia crashes him back to earth, the resulting shame is proportional to the height of the grandiosity. This oscillation (inflation/deflation) is a common pattern in individuals who've had powerful psychedelic experiences and not fully grounded the insights.

2. **Cross-addiction vulnerability** — Even with primary addictions managed, the pattern of using one compulsive behavior to manage emotional states remains. Alcohol may be gone, but the underlying emotional regulation deficit that drove it is an ongoing risk.

3. **Isolation** — He acknowledges being alone, not going out, not accepting invitations. His subconscious literally told him: "You leave this house, talk to people, show yourself to the world." The spiritual path can become a sophisticated justification for avoidance.

4. **Relationship avoidance** — The high standards for a partner ("must activate the Bestia sexually AND share my consciousness level AND have her own purpose") may function as a defense against the vulnerability of actual intimacy. His relational wounds (Naya, Barbara, the crypto circle) remain partially unprocessed.

5. **Project dispersion** — App, book, game, music, foundation, blog, videoblogs, IXNAH system, Nahualismo framework, Hechiceria Practica, social media content. The Vision and Roadmap documents show he's aware of this and has prioritized (app first), but the historical pattern of starting many things and finishing few is a documented risk.

6. **Psychotic risk** — He explicitly references a past depressive psychosis from over-inhabiting the spiritual state. Anyone who uses DMT regularly and practices active imagination at the depth he describes should maintain awareness of this boundary. His current approach (grounded in physical training, employed, structured routines) is protective.
