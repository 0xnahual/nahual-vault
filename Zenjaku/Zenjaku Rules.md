- No roadmap.  
- No engagement farming.  
- No explaining too much—let people figure it out.  
- Mystery > Marketing.

- If the art isn’t **fire**, it doesn’t go out.  
- No rushed drops.  
- Quality over quantity—each piece should feel **effortless but elite.**  

## **3️⃣ Effortless & Fun to Make.**  
- If it ever feels like a chore, stop.  
- No forced updates.  
- No pressure—**only create when it’s naturally fun.**

- Supply shrinks, value shifts.  
- Market dynamics control supply—**no manual burns by collectors.**  
- If trading volume stops, it stops. If it pumps, it pumps.  

## **5️⃣ Zero Responsibilities.**  
- This is not a job.

- No one is “in charge”—the experiment just happens.

- Trading = burns = deflation = value.  
- Flippers & holders are both part of the game.

## **7️⃣ Let People Overthink It.**  
- Never explain too much.  
- The less you say, the more people speculate.  
- If someone calls it **“the most genius NFT ever,” just nod and say nothing.**  

## **8️⃣ The Brand is “IDK Where This is Going.”**  
- That’s the whole point.  
- If someone asks what it’s about, just say **"idk bro, it’s just happening."**  
- The uncertainty **is** the brand.

- Controlled expansion **only if** deflation makes it untradeable.  
- No random dilutions—if new supply is added, it has to make sense.
