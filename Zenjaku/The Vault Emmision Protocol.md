## **The Vault Emission Protocol (VEP)”**

You create a subsystem with five properties:
### **1. It has a fixed emission schedule**

Examples:
- 1 Zenjaku emitted every 24 hours
- or 7 per week 
- or 13 per state cycle
- or 1 per 10 donations
- or 1 per burn event

You choose ONE formula.
It must be **mechanical** and **cold**.

No emotions.
No exceptions.

### **2. The recipients are determined by a RULE, not by you**

Examples:
- **Top trader of that cycle**
- **Most burned Zenjakus**
- **Random holder (provably random)**
- **The address that triggered the latest state change**
- **The address with the oldest unlisted Zenjaku**
- **The first wallet to buy after a burn**
- **Any wallet holding exactly 1 Zenjaku**
    
Pick ONE rule.
The system picks.

Not you.

### **3. Emissions are logged by the bot**

  

Every emission becomes a **state change log**, e.g.:
State emission — 011
Vault release: 1 unit
Recipient index: 0x0a42...
Protocol balance: 387 units

### **4. The vault becomes part of the myth**

  

This transforms the 400 supply from:

  

“extras” → **sacred inventory**

“leftovers” → **system fuel**

“bags” → **lifeblood of the protocol**

  

### **5. You never manually touch it.**

  

ALL transfers are triggered by:

- cron
    
- script
    
- bot
    
- rule
    
- condition
    
- on-chain event
    

  

Never by you making a decision.

  

This preserves aura
